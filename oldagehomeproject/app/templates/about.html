About Us

Welcome to the Old Age House, where we provide a nurturing and caring environment for senior citizens. With a range of services and amenities tailored to the needs of older adults, we strive to create a home-like atmosphere where residents can enjoy their golden years with dignity and respect. Visit us today to experience the warm and welcoming environment of the Old Age House. We are dedicated to providing exceptional care and creating a supportive community for our residents.



Our Mission

To provide holistic, Person Centered Care that enables elders to have the highest quality of life.To provide independence to senior citizens in daily life, and to re-establish high values of life nurtured by them.

Our Vision

We are expert in assisted living and dementia care To be one of the leading global experts in assisted living and dementia care
